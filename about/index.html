About AI Floor Plan Generator

AI Floor Plan Generator is a free online tool that uses artificial intelligence to create professional floor plans and room layouts. Our mission is to make design tools accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill or budget.

Whether you are an interior designer, architect, real estate agent, or homeowner planning a renovation, our tool helps you visualize room layouts in seconds. Simply describe the room, pick a room type, and let AI do the rest.

We support a wide range of room types including living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, bathrooms, offices, studios, and dining rooms. All generated floor plans are free to download and use for personal projects.

The tool runs entirely in your browser. No signup required, no API key needed, and no limits on how many floor plans you can create. Your descriptions are sent directly to Pollinations AI for image generation, and no personal data is stored on our servers.
